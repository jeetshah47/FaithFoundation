Tobacco Free Educational Institutions (ToFEI)

Sr.No Criteria Yes/No Upload Document(If any*)
1. Display of 'Tobacco Free Area' Signage inside the premise of Educational Institute at all prominent place(s)

2. Display of "Tobacco Free Education Institution" signage at entrance/ boundary wall of Educational Institute.

3. The name/designation/contact number are mentioned / updated in the signage

4. No evidence of use of tobacco products inside the premise i.e. cigarette/beedi butts or discarded gutka/tobacco pouches, spitting spots.

5. Poster or other awareness materials on harms of tobacco displayed in the premise.

6. Organisation of at least one tobacco control activity during last 6 months.

7. Designation of Tobacco Monitors and their names, designations, and contact number are mentioned on the signages.

8. Inclusion of "No Tobacco Use" norm in the El's code of conduct guidelines Marking of 100 yards area from the outer.

9. limit of boundary wall / fence of the El. No shops selling tobacco products within 100 yards of the Educational Institute.
